## Per-primitive breakdown (main text)

To verify that VISTA’s gains are not driven by a small subset of tasks, we report a **per-primitive breakdown** across the 12 primitives/tasks used in our benchmark. Figure 14 plots the per-primitive improvement in HQCR (Pass@1) relative to Agent-Q.

Across primitives, the improvements are **mostly positive** and of similar magnitude, indicating that VISTA’s gains are **broad-based** rather than concentrated in a few outlier tasks. The zero line makes regressions immediately visible; we do not observe catastrophic drops on any primitive.

**Figure 14: Per-primitive improvement (ΔHQCR).** ΔHQCR = HQCR\_VISTA − HQCR\_{Agent-Q} for each primitive (Pass@1); zero line indicates no change.
